Service Industry Hiring

Dead Simple.

An easy to use portal for:

    Employers to find staff, and set up interviews with a click of a button.

    Employees to submit their application once and have interview offers roll in.

    Everyone to save time and money.



The Problem:

    In 2016 the annual turnover rate in the service industry was 73%. 1.

    Businesses simply cannot afford to waste time and resources finding staff.

    Professionals seeking employment often need employment ASAP.

1. JOLTS

The answer:

    A local, single purpose, easy to use app connecting Employers with Staff

    No wasted resources tracking down craigslist repsponses, emailed applications, and running ads

    No wasted time filling out a miriad of applcations to dozens of business with different forms

Features:

    A simple search to list apllicants with the exact positions and availabilities needed.

    One click to send a custom tailored text message to any applicants to set up an interview.

    Favorite applicants for later.

    Delete any applicants you're not interested in.
